Marilith

The Marilith, also known as the Queen of the Succubi, is a fearsome demoness in Dungeons and Dragons lore. With the torso of a powerfully built humanoid and the lower body of a massive serpent, the Marilith wields a lethal combination of six razor-sharp swords with deadly precision. Known for her cunning and strategic prowess, she commands legions of demonic minions in the eternal battle between the forces of chaos and order. As one of the most iconic demon lords in D&D, the Marilith strikes fear into the hearts of adventurers brave enough to face her in combat.
